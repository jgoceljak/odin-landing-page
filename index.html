    • Hitori Gotoh
    • Lead Guitar

    • Ikuyo Kita
    • Vocals/Rhythm Guitar
    • Fearing her own life was shallow and pointless, Kita Ikuyo joined the band in an attempt to create meaning for herself and to form deep bonds with those around her
    • Nijika Ichiji
    • Drums
    • Nijika Ichiji created Kessoku Band to realize her dream of making her sister's livehouse so famous that even her late mother would be able to notice them from the stars above.
    • Ryo Yamada
    • Bass
    • Ryo Yamada, discontent with the direction of her old band, joined Kessoku Band to create a unique sound of her own, and to walk a different path than her rich white collar parents.
